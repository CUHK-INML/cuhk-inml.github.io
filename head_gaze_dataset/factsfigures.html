The Head and Gaze VR Behavior Motion Dataset is a large-scale dataset containing both the head motion, which denotes FoV, and eye gaze information.

  • It contains 180, 000 sequences of head and gaze tracking data, represented as coordinate and quaternion along with corresponding images.
  • The data sequences are captured among 100 users with high sampling frequency 120Hz.
  • It provides 27 videos with 9 dimensions, which contains three technique metrics: camera motion, video quality, and the dispersion of region of interest (ROI).

With those annotations, VR behavior is designed to support tasks including:

  • 360-degree Video Streaming
  • User Identification
  • Psycho Analysis
  • Video Recommdentation
